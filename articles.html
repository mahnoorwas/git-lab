FOR NOBEL PRIZE

Abdus Salam's research focused on particle physics, particularly the unification of fundamental forces. His most significant work was in electroweak theory for which he shared the 1979 Nobel Prize in Physics....,He also made contributions to quantum field theory, grand unified theories, and supersymmetry, and helped develop Pakistan's scientific infrastructure.

FOR NISHAN-E-IMTIAZ

Abdus Salam's work that earned him the Nishan-e-Imtiaz (Pakistan's highest civilian award) in 1979 was his theoretical unification of the weak and electromagnetic forces...,for which he shared the 1979 Nobel Prize in Physics with Sheldon Glashow and Steven Weinberg. This unification, known as the electroweak theory, predicted the existence of weak neutral currents and W and Z particles before their experimental discovery.

FOR ADAMS PRIZE
